Privacy Policy

Our commitment to player safety and data protection

Introduction

Isleham United Athena's ("we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our players. This policy explains how we handle personal data on this website in accordance with UK GDPR.

What Data We Collect

We only display data for which we have received explicit written parental consent. This includes:

  • First Names: We never publish surnames.
  • Photographs: Action shots or portraits of players.
  • Football Details: Player positions and squad roles.

How We Protect the Children

To ensure the highest level of safeguarding, we implement the following rules:

  • No Identifiable Metadata: We strip location data (EXIF) from images before uploading.
  • No Personal Contact Info: We never publish addresses, school names, or birthdays.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell or share player data with any external marketing companies.

Your Rights & Consent

Parents and guardians have the "Right to be Forgotten." If you wish for your child's image or name to be removed from this website, please contact the coaching staff. We will remove the content within 3 working days.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please reach out via our Contact Page.
