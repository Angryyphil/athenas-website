Welcome to the Athena's Hub

To keep things simple for our parents and players, you can find all our standard club info, policies, and frequently asked questions below.

General Information

Training: Thursday Nights; 18:00 - 19:30.
Summer sessions are held at the Isleham Recreation Ground (CB7 5RY) behind The Beeches Cafe.
Winter sessions are held at Red Lodge Sports Paviliion (IP28 8FQ) on the Astro Turf.

Matchdays: Matches are played on Saturday mornings. All Home games are held at The Isleham Recreation Ground (CB7 5RY).

Weather Policy

In the event of bad weather, the coaching staff will inspect the pitch and notify the parents' group chat directly if the game is to be called off. If you haven't heard from us, assume the game is on!

Parent FAQs

Can my daughter try a session before joining?

Absolutely! We offer two free trial sessions so she can get a feel for the team, the coaches and the other girls before making any commitment.

What equipment does she need to start?

Shin pads and a water bottle are mandatory for safety at all times. We recommend molded football boots, as they are suitable for both the summer grass pitches and the winter Astro Turf.

What do the annual club fees cover?

Club fees cover FA player insurance, league registration, winter pitch hire and their official matchday kit.

Anything else?

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us via our Contact Form.
