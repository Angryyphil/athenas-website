Join the Team

Welcome to the Club!

We are a community-focused team based in Isleham, dedicated to developing young talent in a fun, supportive and safe environment.

Whether you are a beginner or the next Lioness, there is a place for you here.

The Athena's Mission

To have competitive teams playing football the right way and to combine sporting success, fitness and a sociable environment.

We want our girls to avoid injury, score a few goals, not let in too many and have enough energy left to enjoy the rest of our weekends!

We train Thursday nights during the winter months at Red Lodge Sports Pavilion (IP28 8FQ) on the Astro Turf.

Home games and training during the summer months are played at The Beeches, Isleham on the main recreation ground with kick-offs usually around 09:00am on Saturday mornings.

Post-match analysis, exaggeration and rehydration is often held in the Beeches Cafe.

Get Involved

We are always looking for new players and volunteers so feel free to come along to training or contact one of the coaches via the Contact Form for more information.

We are active on social media and have pages on both Facebook and Instagram where match reports, news and pictures are shared.
