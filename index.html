Join the Team

Welcome to the Club!

We are a local team based in Isleham, dedicated to developing young talent in a fun, supportive and safe environment.

Whether you are a beginner or the next Lioness, there is a place for you here.

The Athena's Mission

To have our teams playing football the right way and to combine sporting success, fitness and a sociable environment.

We want our girls to have fun, score a few goals, not let too many in and have enough energy left to enjoy the rest of the weekend!

Home games and training during the summer months take place at The Beeches, Isleham on the main recreation ground on Saturday mornings.

We train Thursday nights during the winter months at Red Lodge Sports Pavilion on the Astro Turf.

Post-match analysis, exaggeration and rehydration is often held in the Beeches Cafe.

Get Involved

We are always looking for new players and volunteers so feel free to come along to training or contact one of the coaches via the Contact Form for more information.

We are active on social media and have pages on both Facebook and Instagram where match reports, news and pictures are shared.
